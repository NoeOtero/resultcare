The ResultCare application is designed to help physicians and other healthcare providers deliver the highest quality care at the right price. We deliver real-time cutting edge research evidence and cost information to guide better clinical decisions.

OUR VISON

The ResultCare app searches the latest literature and allows healthcare providers to utilize the most accurate diagnostic work up based on published results.

Our inspiration comes from our years of experience in medical research and clinical medicine. Our mission is to use our knowledge to transform medical practice to provide patients with the best care possible.

Evidence based medicine or EBM is about utilizing research data that allows medical practice to be reproducible. Our goal is to provide physicians with best practice guidelines bringing the most up to date evidence to the point of care.
